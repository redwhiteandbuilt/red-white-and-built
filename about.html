About Red White & Built

Red White & Built is a community‑driven marketplace dedicated to celebrating and promoting American manufacturing. Born from the idea that our nation’s supply chain should be transparent, resilient and proudly made in the USA, we connect manufacturers, suppliers, brands, contractors and retailers under one roof.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of storytelling, craftsmanship and domestic production. By highlighting the people and processes behind the goods we use every day, we hope to inspire confidence in American‑made products and strengthen local economies.

What We Do

  • Verify authenticity: Our verification process ensures that every listing on the platform meets strict criteria for domestic production and ethical practices.
  • Foster connections: We make it simple for buyers to discover suppliers, for manufacturers to find trusted vendors and for brands to partner with retailers.
  • Provide resources: From training courses on compliance and sourcing to tools for managing contracts and requests for proposals, we equip businesses with the knowledge to grow.

Whether you’re a small artisan or a large contractor, Red White & Built welcomes you to join our movement and help build a brighter future for American industry.
